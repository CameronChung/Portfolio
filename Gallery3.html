"Routine": A Late Night Walk

"Routine", 2021, Premiere Pro, 5 mins.



This video is a visual and sensational experience that demonstrates the view point of a women walking alone through various times of the night. With raw images and audio, you too experience it with the character. The dangers of being a women in society is starting to bring to light the topic of conversation within social and mass media. Filming this in my own day to day life opens my eyes to the never ending caution women have to keep while out in public. This is my representation and interpretation of women's everyday lives.




"LITTLE LIGHTS"

"Little Lights", 2021, p5.js



"Little Lights" is an interactive javascript activity that shows different moving components as the user holds down the right mouse click and drags it across the screen. Play around with it yourself; see the different changes in colors and shapes from all different sides of the square!
