"My Avatar", 2022, Blender and Adobe 3D Substance Painter

"My Avatar" is an original character design 3D model that was built, sculpted, and painted using Blender and Adobe's 3D Substance Painter. This piece touches on the topic about holding onto childhood and the things that heal the inner child within you. One thing that I tend to still gravitate towards are the little trinkets and toys that contain the sole purpose of accompanying you. Family and friends ridicule me for collecting these as "you are too old to be buying them" yet they bring me an abundance of joy. Should you have to sacrifice your happiness at the mere judgment of another?




"LITTLE LIGHTS"

"Little Lights", 2021, p5.js

"Little Lights" is an interactive javascript activity that shows different moving components as the user holds down the left mouse click and drags it across the screen. This piece is for everyone to play no matter the demographic. Play around with it yourself on the right hand side; see the different changes in colors and shapes from all different sides of the square!




"My Everything Bagel"



"My Everything Bagel", 2021, p5.js

"My Everything Bagel" is an interactive javascript 3D environment that incorporate auto rotating 3D objects as the user holds down the left mouse click and drags it across the screen. This piece demonstrates the presense of people in an online environment. As online users everything choose an avatar to represent them or an online persona, I coded this activty to represent myself. Drawing inspiration from Academy Award Winning film "Everything Everywhere All At Once", Play around with it yourself on the right hand side; see the different changes in colors and shapes from all different sides of the square!




"PHLAPPY TURTLE"

"Phlappy Turtle", 2023, Unity

"Phlappy Turtle" is a short 2D platformer game that challenges the players skills and figuring a way to win. Navigating in the dark with limited vision, it is all up to strategy. In making this game, I learned many aspects like game development, team management, and practice time management. With a project like this, I was able to work towards set milestones while adapting to changes along the way. This process taught me patience and open mindedness as I navigated this new program.






CADRE MENTORSHIP FALL20' SHOWCASE

"Fragile Dreams", 2020, HTML and CSS

"Fragile Dreams" is a digital installation in a virtual art gallery (New Art City) made within the Cadre Mentorship Program. With the help of Mentor Raymond Lam and Partner Cindy Tran, we were able to create a tribute piece for the Late Qing Han known as QuinniArt on Instagram. Her work inspired hundreds of thousands of people around the world. My mentorship pairing wanted to give this piece a new platform to further spread her love of art. Preserving her original design, we created an interactive web page to experience the full scope of senses with Quinni's work.
